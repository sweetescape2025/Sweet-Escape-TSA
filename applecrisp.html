Best Apple Crisp

  • Here's a simple and delicious Apple Crisp recipe you can try:

    Ingredients:

      For the apple filling:
    • 6-8 medium apples (such as Granny Smith or Honeycrisp), peeled, cored, and sliced
    • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
    • 1/2 cup granulated sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
    • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour (optional, to help thicken the filling)
    For the crisp topping:
    • 1 cup old-fashioned rolled oats
    • 3/4 cup all-purpose flour
    • 1/2 cup packed brown sugar
    • 1/2 teaspoon ground cinnamon
    • 1/4 teaspoon salt
    • 1/2 cup unsalted butter, cold and cut into small cubes
    • 1/2 cup chopped nuts (optional, such as walnuts or pecans)

    Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350°F (175°C).
      2. Prepare the apple filling:
    2. In a large bowl, toss the sliced apples with lemon juice, sugar, cinnamon, nutmeg, and flour.
    3. Pour the apple mixture into a greased 9x9-inch baking dish (or similar size).
      4. Prepare the crisp topping:
    4. In a separate bowl, combine the oats, flour, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt.
    5. Add the cold cubed butter and use a pastry cutter or your fingers to work the butter into the dry ingredients until the mixture resembles coarse crumbs. If you’re adding nuts, stir them in now.
      6. Assemble the crisp:
    6. Evenly spread the oat topping over the apples in the baking dish. Bake the crisp:
    7. Bake for 40-45 minutes, or until the apples are tender and the topping is golden brown and crispy.
    8. You can check the apples by piercing them with a fork—if they’re soft, the crisp is ready.
      9. Serve:
    9. Let it cool for a few minutes before serving. Enjoy it warm with a scoop of vanilla ice cream or a dollop of whipped cream!
      10. Tips:
      For extra flavor, you can add a splash of vanilla extract or a pinch of ground ginger to the filling. Feel free to use a mix of apple varieties for a more complex flavor.
