Hot Chocolate

A simple and delicious hot chocolate recipe

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups whole milk (or any milk of your choice)
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder (unsweetened)
  • 1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract (optional)
  • A pinch of salt
  • Whipped cream or marshmallows for topping (optional)
  • Shaved chocolate or chocolate chips (optional, for extra richness)

    • Instructions:

    1. Heat the Milk: In a small saucepan, heat the milk over medium heat. Stir occasionally to prevent the milk from scalding.
    2. Mix the Cocoa and Sugar: While the milk is heating, whisk the cocoa powder, sugar, and salt together in a small bowl.
    3. Combine Cocoa Mixture with Milk: Once the milk is warm (but not boiling), add the cocoa powder and sugar mixture to the milk. Whisk until the cocoa is fully dissolved and the mixture is smooth.
    4. Add Vanilla (Optional): Stir in the vanilla extract for added flavor, if desired.
    5. Serve: Pour the hot chocolate into a mug and top with whipped cream or marshmallows. You can also sprinkle some shaved chocolate or chocolate chips on top for extra richness!
    6. Enjoy! Sip and enjoy your cozy homemade hot chocolate.
