Mug Cake

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup flour
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 2 tablespoons sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • Pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons milk
  • 1 tablespoon melted butter
  • 2 tablespoons chocolate chips

Instructions:

  1. In a microwave-safe mug, mix dry ingredients.
  2. Add wet ingredients and stir until combined.
  3. Microwave on high for 1 minute or until the center is set. Top with whipped cream and chocolate chips.
