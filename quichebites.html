Quiche Bites

Ingredients:

  • 1 sheet puff pastry (cut into small squares)
  • 4 eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk or cream
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (cheddar or Swiss)
  • 1/2 cup cooked bacon or spinach (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F (190°C).
  2. Press puff pastry squares into muffin tin cups.
  3. Whisk eggs with milk, cheese, and seasonings, then pour into pastry.
  4. Bake for 15-20 minutes until set and golden.
