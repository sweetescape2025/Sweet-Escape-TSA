Sugar Popcorn

Ingredients:

  • 1/4 cup popcorn kernels
  • 2 tablespoons melted butter
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon cinnamon

Instructions:

  1. Pop the popcorn using your preferred method.
  2. Drizzle with melted butter and toss with cinnamon and sugar.
  3. Serve immediately for a sweet, warm treat.
