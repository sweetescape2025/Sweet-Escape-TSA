Winter Trail Mix

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup mixed nuts (almonds, cashews, walnuts)
  • 1 cup dried cranberries or cherries
  • 1/2 cup dark chocolate chips
  • 1/4 cup sunflower seeds
  • 1/2 cup mini pretzels

Instructions:

    Prepare Ingredients:
  1. If you havenâ€™t done so already, chop or break down larger ingredients (e.g., dried fruit into smaller pieces, pretzels into bite-size bits, nuts if you prefer smaller pieces). Mix Ingredients:
  2. In a large bowl, combine the nuts, dried fruits, seeds, grains, and chocolate. You can adjust the proportions based on your preferences, but a good balance is roughly 1 part nuts, 1 part dried fruits, and 1 part other add-ins (seeds, pretzels, etc.).
    3. Add Flavor (Optional):
  3. If you want, sprinkle in a little cinnamon or nutmeg for extra warmth or lightly season with salt for a contrast to the sweetness.
    4. Store:
  4. Once mixed, transfer your trail mix into an airtight container or individual snack bags. It can be kept at room temperature for several weeks, making it perfect for grabbing and going on a winter hike, or as a quick snack during the day.
